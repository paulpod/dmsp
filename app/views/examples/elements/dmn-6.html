Health conditions and driving

90% of drivers who inform us of a medical condition keep their licence.

You must surrender your licence to DVLA if your doctor tells you to stop driving or if you do not meet the required standard for driving because of your medical condition.

Surrendering your licence voluntarily may mean you can start driving again sooner.
