Driving with a disability or a health condition

    Health Conditions and driving

    Reapply for a driving licence following a medical condition

    Driving and Transport

Notify using the new service beta

This is a 'beta' service - find out what this means for you.

To notify us online you will need:

  • Your 16 character drivers number on your drivers licence
  • Your National Insurance Number
  • The Categories you are entitled to drive
  • Your GP's phone number and Postcode
  • Your Consultant's phone number and Postcode
  • Medication names and dosage
  • A valid e-mail address
Start now

You can only use this service if you are telling us about your medical condition

Contact us on the number below if you wish to inform us of medical conditions relating to somebody else.
